Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ledger Pail Kids?

Ledger Pail Kids is a satirical digital collectible project built on the XRPL. Each NFT is a 1/1 with on-chain utility for holders.

What do I get when I mint?

You get a 1/1 collectible plus access to our XRP faucet (earn-while-you-hold) and an equal $PAIL airdrop to all holders after mint. Faucet payouts are in XRP; the airdrop distributes $PAIL.

How will holders earn $XRP?

After sell-out, we run weekly epochs at a fixed time. At each snapshot, we split the faucet pool for that epoch equally among NFTs in the snapshot and send XRP to those addresses via standard payment transactions. If you sell before a snapshot, you aren’t counted for that epoch; the new holder is eligible going forward. The faucet pool is funded by yield harvested (when available) from our XLS-30 $PAIL/XRP AMM position—converted to XRP—plus a fixed share of treasury and secondary royalties.

Is there a roadmap?

No traditional roadmap — utilities are shipping. We build in public, iterate with the community, and keep mechanics XRPL-native.
